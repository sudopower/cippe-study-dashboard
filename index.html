    About this dashboard

    Local study app for the IAPP CIPP/E exam. All data is loaded from local JS files and progress is stored in your browser's localStorage. Nothing is sent over the network.

    • Practice — untimed walk through MCQs at your own pace, see explanations after each.
    • Quiz — graded run of a configurable number of randomised MCQs with a final score.
    • Progress — review your history and reset if needed.
